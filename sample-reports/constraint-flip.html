The Flip

What it signals: Customers mention speed of response as top differentiator in reviews
What it enables: Ship in hours not months; every customer talks to a founder; zero bureaucracy
Constraint-Driven Ideas
• Public changelog showing daily shipping velocity
• 24-hour feature request turnaround guarantee
• Direct Slack channel with the engineering team for every customer
• Customer co-design sessions — build features live with users
The Moat Idea
Make shipping speed and customer intimacy the core brand — competitors with 50 engineers cannot match your responsiveness.
